Next search Catalog participation agreement¶

Each year Next Search Catalog libraries are asked to commit to particpating in Next Search Catalog for the next calendar year by reviewing and signing the Next Search Catalog particpation agreement.

Where to find:¶

  1. Log in to https://staff.nextkansas.org

  2. Do a catalog search for “Next particpation agreement”

  3. When the record loads, click on the “Next participation agreement” link for the current year
