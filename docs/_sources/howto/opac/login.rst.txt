How to log into an account in the OPAC
======================================

#. Open https://nextkansas.org
#. Click on the icon that says "Log into your account"
#. Enter your username or your library card number and your password in the appropriate boxes and click on "Log in"
#. If you're successful you will be taken to an account summary page:
#. If you enter an incorrect login/password combination, you can use the "Forgot your password" feature or you can contact your library for help
