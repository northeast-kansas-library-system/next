Director accounts¶

Finding the “Director” account for your library¶

  1. Log into Koha with your library’s tech or circ account and do a checkout search for “Director”

  2. Find your library’s director account in the list of accounts

  3. Go to the details page of the account and note the cardnumber and the e-mail address
    • If the e-mail address is incorrect, click on edit and enter the correct e-mail address for your directors

  4. Go to the OPAC at nextkansas.org and click on “Log into your account”

  5. In the pop-up window, click on “Forgot your password”

  6. Enter your card number and your director account’s e-mail address and click “Submit”

  7. You should see a page telling you you’re about to receive an e-mail

  8. Open your director account’s e-mail from Next Kansas

  9. Enter your new password in the appropriate field and click “Submit”
    • After January 16, 2022, staff and library associate accounts will require strong and complex passwords

  10. Log into the staff client with your new password

  11. Success!
